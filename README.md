# Suika Game

A recreation of the Suika (Watermelon) Game built in Unity and playable in the browser.

Drop fruits into the container and match pairs to combine them into bigger fruits. Try to make it all the way to a watermelon without overflowing the container.

## Play Now

**[Play Suika Game](https://snadjimi.github.io/Suika-Game/)**

## How to Play

- Move around with WASD
- Click to drop a fruit into the container
- When two of the same fruit collide, they merge into the next fruit
- Game over when fruits go above the container

## Fruit Evolution Chain

Cherry > Strawberry > Grapes > Dekopon > Orange > Apple > Pear > Peach > Pineapple > Melon > Watermelon
